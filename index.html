Southern Archipelago

Tram 11 → Saltholmen Ferries: Styrsö • Donsö • Vrångö • Brännö Swim spots & coastal trails
  • Morning: Tram 11 to Saltholmen terminal.
  • Ferry to Styrsö or Vrångö for walks & picnic.
  • Late afternoon ferry back; dinner in Linné.
Ferry tips — Saltholmen → Islands
  • Ticket: A 24-hour or 3-day Västtrafik ticket covers trams + archipelago ferries (buy in the app).
  • Timing: Trams to Saltholmen run ~every 7–10 min; ferries are ~15–45 min depending on island/route.
  • Boarding: On sunny days queues form early; catch an earlier ferry out and a mid-afternoon return.
  • Weather: Wind can affect departures; check live times the night before and morning of.
  • Essentials: Layers, water, and swim shoes help for rocky entries.

Liseberg & Avenyn

Stop: Korsvägen Trams: 2 / 4 / 5 / 6 Liseberg Amusement Park Avenyn (Kungsportsavenyn) Dinner: SK Mat & Människor

  • Evening stroll up Avenyn; galleries, bars, and neon.
